\# GarageAI Vision



Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: GarageAI Engineering Team



\---



\# Mission



Build an enterprise-grade Garage Management Platform powered by Artificial Intelligence that improves customer experience, automates business processes, and provides a scalable foundation for future AI capabilities.



\---



\# Vision



GarageAI is not an AI application.



GarageAI is a Garage Management System with Artificial Intelligence as a business capability.



Artificial Intelligence exists to support the business, not replace it.



\---



\# Problem Statement



Most garage management systems provide traditional CRUD operations but lack intelligent assistance.



Garage owners spend significant time:



\- answering repetitive customer questions

\- creating bookings

\- checking availability

\- explaining services

\- searching historical information



Artificial Intelligence can automate these repetitive tasks while keeping the human in control.



\---



\# Business Goals



GarageAI should enable customers to:



\- Ask questions naturally

\- Book services using conversation

\- Receive instant answers

\- Upload images for analysis

\- Communicate using voice

\- Receive intelligent recommendations



GarageAI should enable garage staff to:



\- Save time

\- Reduce repetitive work

\- Improve customer satisfaction

\- Increase booking conversion

\- Improve operational efficiency



\---



\# Technical Goals



GarageAI will be designed as an enterprise AI platform using:



\- Domain Driven Design (DDD)

\- Vertical Slice Architecture

\- CQRS

\- Clean Architecture

\- Evolutionary Architecture



The platform must support multiple AI providers without changing business logic.



\---



\# AI Capabilities



The platform will evolve to support:



\- Conversation

\- Voice

\- Vision

\- Knowledge (RAG)

\- Tool Calling

\- Automation



These capabilities are independent from any AI provider.



\---



\# Long-Term Vision



GarageAI should become a reusable AI platform.



Future business domains may include:



\- Healthcare

\- Legal

\- Finance

\- Human Resources



Only the business modules should change.



The AI platform should remain reusable.



\---



\# Success Criteria



GarageAI is considered successful when:



\- AI can create bookings through conversation.

\- AI can answer customer questions.

\- AI can call business tools.

\- AI supports multiple providers.

\- AI supports voice interactions.

\- AI supports knowledge retrieval (RAG).

\- The architecture remains maintainable as the platform evolves.



\---



\# Non-Goals



GarageAI is NOT intended to:



\- replace garage employees

\- build technology for the sake of technology

\- become tied to a single AI provider

\- depend on one framework

\- sacrifice architecture for short-term development speed



\---



\# Guiding Principle



Business drives technology.



Technology never drives the business.
