\# GarageAI Roadmap



Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Owner: GarageAI Engineering Team



\---



\# Purpose



This roadmap defines the planned evolution of GarageAI from a traditional business application into an enterprise AI platform.



The roadmap is iterative.



Each sprint delivers working software.



Architecture evolves only when justified by business requirements.



\---



\# Phase 1 — Foundation ✅



Status: Completed



Objectives



\- Define project vision

\- Establish architecture

\- Create engineering standards

\- Create AI platform design

\- Create ADRs

\- Prepare project structure



Deliverables



\- Vision

\- Architecture

\- Engineering Rules

\- AI Platform

\- ADR-001

\- ADR-002



\---



\# Phase 2 — AI Conversation 🚧



Objective



Deliver the first working AI conversation.



Deliverables



\- OpenAI SDK integration

\- AI Orchestrator

\- Conversation endpoint

\- Basic prompt execution

\- Dependency Injection

\- Configuration

\- End-to-end testing



Definition of Done



A user sends a message.



GarageAI returns an AI response.



\---



\# Phase 3 — Conversation History



Objective



Introduce persistent conversations.



Deliverables



\- Conversation storage

\- Session management

\- Message history

\- Context-aware conversations



\---



\# Phase 4 — Tool Calling



Objective



Allow AI to execute business actions.



Examples



\- Create Booking

\- Check Availability

\- Find Customer

\- Search Vehicle



The AI will move from answering questions to performing work.



\---



\# Phase 5 — Knowledge (RAG)



Objective



Allow AI to answer questions using company knowledge.



Knowledge Sources



\- Service manuals

\- Company documentation

\- FAQs

\- Policies



Deliverables



\- Embeddings

\- Vector Database

\- Azure AI Search (or equivalent)

\- Retrieval pipeline



\---



\# Phase 6 — Voice



Objective



Enable natural voice conversations.



Capabilities



\- Speech to Text

\- Text to Speech

\- Real-time voice interaction



\---



\# Phase 7 — Vision



Objective



Allow AI to understand images.



Examples



\- Vehicle inspection

\- Damage detection

\- Document understanding



\---



\# Phase 8 — Multi-Provider Support



Objective



Support multiple AI providers.



Examples



\- OpenAI

\- Azure OpenAI

\- Ollama



Business features remain unchanged.



\---



\# Phase 9 — Enterprise Platform



Objective



Transform GarageAI into a reusable enterprise AI platform.



Future Domains



\- Healthcare

\- Finance

\- HR

\- Legal



Only business modules should change.



The AI platform should remain reusable.



\---



\# Success Criteria



GarageAI should demonstrate:



\- Enterprise Architecture

\- DDD

\- Vertical Slice

\- CQRS

\- Clean Architecture

\- AI Orchestration

\- Tool Calling

\- RAG

\- Voice

\- Vision

\- Multi-provider AI



\---



\# Long-Term Goal



GarageAI should be a portfolio-quality enterprise AI platform that demonstrates both software engineering excellence and practical AI integration.



The project should prepare its contributors to design, build, and maintain production-grade AI systems.
